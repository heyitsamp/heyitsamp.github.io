Tap Out is a little mobile game we made quite by accident while doing something else. Since it was fun, we published it! The goal is to make as many taps as you can in five seconds. It's great for challenging friends with newer and better techniques and then bringing them together around a larger phone or tablet for even higher scores.

Tap Out has been through three versions, the first two under another name. Version one was implemented in PhoneGap. Version two switched to Unity. Seth had that finished a week after picking up Unity and C# for the first time. Version three was a UX pass and brought the game to it's current state where we find people can just pick it up and play with no extra explanation.
