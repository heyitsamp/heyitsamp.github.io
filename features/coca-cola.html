The assignment was to design a Coke bottle that could be consumed while driving a car without tilting the bottle. Keeping it simple, the solutions was a plastic bottle that maintained the classic Coke bottle shape and brand, within the bottle a plastic bag like membrane. Upon consumption via sucking through the modified cap, the inner membrane would collapse and deliver the soda. This solution was recognized by Coca Cola as one of the top 3 solutions submitted from over 25 design firms.
